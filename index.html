// 01 — Background

A little about me

I'm a first-year Computer Science student at Trinity College Dublin, originally from Magnitogorsk, Russia. My journey into tech started early — I was a prize-winner of three Math Olympiads and four CS Olympiads at different levels, which shaped the way I think about problems.

Before arriving at TCD, I earned a professional diploma as an Electronic Computer Operator from MSTU Nosov in Magnitogorsk, covering databases, front-end development, and Python. I then completed the International Foundation Programme (IFP) at TCD, studying Math, Physics, EAP, and Introduction to Programming.

Outside of code, I rode horses competitively for five years — earning my second adult category and over 20 competition medals. I also acted in school theatre, edited event videos, and volunteered extensively in my community.
